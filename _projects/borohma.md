**International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) Competition** *Dry Lab Sub-Team Lead (March – October 2024)*

Our iGEM team investigated the bioproduction of a camphor-based insect repellent designed to replace traditional, potentially harmful chemical compounds. As the Dry Lab Sub-Team Lead, I spearheaded the computational modeling and digital infrastructure for the project.

### Key Contributions:
* **Diffusion Modeling:** Developed a transmembrane diffusion model to accurately quantify compound release rates from minicell encapsulations.
* **Market Forecasting:** Engineered a Bass diffusion model using Python to forecast the market adoption curve for our dual-function mosquito-repellent fragrance.
* **Web Development:** Coded the dry lab technical documentation and built interactive data visualizations for the team's official informational website.
